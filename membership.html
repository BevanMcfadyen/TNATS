Membership

Membership of the Toodyay Naturalists’ Club Inc. (TNC) is open to any person or corporation interested in attaining or promoting the objects of the Association.

There are 7 classes of membership, and they are:

  1. Ordinary Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  2. Junior Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  3. Life Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  4. Family Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  5. Body Corporate Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  6. Concessional Member – a member who pays the Subscription Fees and does not fall into the other classes of membership;
  7. Honorary Life Member (elected only)
